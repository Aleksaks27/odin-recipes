Milkshake

Description

Though this beverage takes minimal skill and ingredients to create, it is true dairy heaven that will satisfy and refresh everyone.

This milkshake is perfect as a complement to main meals, such as pizza and chicken, or even just on its own. Either way, be sure to try this out today!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Any flavourings you like, for example, fruit or chocolate

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients and place them into a blender.
  2. Blend everything together until you achieve a smooth and thick texture.
  3. Serve and enjoy while still cold.
